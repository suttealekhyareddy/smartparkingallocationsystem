Efficient Smart Parking Solution

This Smart Parking Allocation System assigns vehicles to suitable parking slots based on vehicle size, priority level, distance, and slot availability using CSP techniques.

Constraints Used

Size Constraint

Vehicles are assigned slots based on vehicle dimensions.

Priority Constraint

VIP and emergency vehicles get nearest parking slots.

Distance Constraint

Nearest available slots are preferred for efficiency.

Uniqueness Constraint

Only one vehicle can occupy one slot at a time.
